Is Physics-Chat free to use?

Yes, Physics-Chat is completely free to use. However, some features may have limited free access and require tokens to use. Tokens can be earned by reporting bugs or purchased through the platform.

What are tokens?

Tokens are a virtual currency used on Physics-Chat that you can use to ask questions. Each question or answer costs a certain number of tokens to submit, and users can acquire tokens buy purchasing or referring new users to the platform.

Can I ask questions about topics other than physics?

Yes! Despite the name, Physics-Chat is not limited to physics-related topics. You can ask questions about any subject you like, as long as they comply with our community guidelines.

How do I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or issues with the platform, you can contact our customer support team through the contact page or by email. We will do our best to assist you as quickly as possible.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, contact us from the contact page or by email. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
