Text analysis

For example, if someone asks our AI to analyze the sentiment of the following sentence: "I had an amazing time at the beach today!", our AI can determine that the sentiment is positive by recognizing words like "amazing" and "time" which are associated with positive sentiment. The result of the analysis could be represented as a sentiment score between -1 and 1, where -1 represents negative sentiment, 0 represents neutral sentiment, and 1 represents positive sentiment. In this case, the sentiment score would be close to 1, indicating a highly positive sentiment.
